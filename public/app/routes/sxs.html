IT'S NOT JUST A 1000-CLASS. IT'S A WHOLE NEW CLASS.

The outdoors are meant to be explored. The highest hills, the deepest canyons, and the farthest reaches of the forests all lie in wait. And now, we bring you an entirely new way to experience it all: the Pioneer 1000.

Nothing compares to this flagship model, either in the Honda lineup, or in the industry. And it all starts with a class-leading 999cc twin-cylinder engine. Add in the industry’s first and only 6-speed fully automatic Dual Clutch Transmission, and you get a blend of raw power and driving performance that delivers unmatched capability on the jobsite, and on the trails.

This is the 1000-class you’ve been waiting for. And it’s ready to take you places you never expected.

WE'VE PACKED A LOT OF POWER UNDER THAT HOOD.

The Pioneer 1000 puts everything we’ve learned designing off-road bikes, ATVs and side-by-sides into one extreme package. Like the 1000-pound hauling** and 2000-pound towing capacities, unsurpassed by any competitor. The exclusive QuickFlip® seating system that lets you go from a 3-seater, to 4, and to 5. Fully-independent front and rear suspension with some of the longest travel of any multi-purpose side-by-side. And open or locked front and rear differentials that prevent you from unnecessarily tearing up delicate surfaces.

And let’s remember: this is a Honda, a name that’s long stood for quality and reliability. So you get class-leading power, performance and features, purpose-built by the best in the business.
